Farming Life in Another World

December 27, 2024


Farming Life in Another World (Isekai Nonbiri Nouka) is the only web novel I’ve read, and I absolutely loved it! It’s a wonder, a delight in every sense. Even with some limitations, I read it in Spanish, which is a bit sad because a lot is lost in translation from Japanese to English and then to Spanish. Nonetheless, it was a fascinating experience. I still enjoyed it immensely. Every chapter kept me hooked and craving more. I've only had time to read the first 413 chapters, but I've already downloaded four PDFs with the web novel translated into Spanish with chapters from 414 to 741. I’m very excited to continue. Additionally, I have four more PDFs pre-downloaded with chapters from 742 to 881. So, my adventure is far from over. The following might contain spoilers from the first 80 chapters!


The story is about a boy who is very sick and dies. However, his fate changes radically when God gives him a second chance in another world, along with a replica of a divine dance, though the protagonist doesn’t know it. Equipped with extraordinary abilities, the protagonist is overpowered and has incredible skills. For example, he:


  • Can kill "rabbits," those rabbits that require several soldiers from the demon kingdom to defeat just one.

  • Can kill boars, those boars he kills with a single blow to the head. Hahaha, if a cute rabbit from that forest is crazy, imagine a boar!

  • Encountered two infernal wolves (huge wolves with knife-like horns on their heads) and thought they were hungry puppies, so he gave them boar meat. The wolves were surprised, thinking ("How scary, this guy treats us like normal dogs and gives us boar meat from this forest as if it were nothing.") Those two wolves, named Kuro and Yuki, had kids, and those kids had kids, and the protagonist ended up with a pack of infernal wolves from the great forest of death, enough to conquer a country.

  • Has an army of demon spiders.

  • His first wife is Ruu Rurushi, the Vampire Princess.

  • His second wife is Tier, the Angel of Annihilation.


I won’t give more examples of how incredible it is because I’d end up telling the whole novel. Unfortunately, I haven't read the novel in a long time, so some details escape me. Therefore, I don't remember the details very well, but if I’m very lost, I just need to reread five or six chapters to refresh my memory. Basically, it’s incredible.
