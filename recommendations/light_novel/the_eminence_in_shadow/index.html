The Eminence in Shadow

Publicado el 30 de diciembre de 2024.


>

The Eminence in Shadow (Kage no Jitsuryokusha ni Naritakute!) is a typical isekai genre, as you probably know, with all the clichés you've come to expect, but with one small twist: the protagonist wants to be an eminence in the shadows.

At first, you might think, ("Well, this is pretty cool, but it would be even better if his circle of friends knew instead of treating him like an idiot"), but after a while, you get used to others thinking he's dumb. The girls, however, find his stupidity "charming".

For example, when he is fighting a girl, to appear more stupid, he lets himself get hit and uses bags of blood to pretend he's injured. But he is clueless and doesn't realize that normal people can't be used as punching bags and then just walk away. The girl, on the other hand, is like ("Even though I'm practically torturing him, he doesn't give up.")

One sad thing about it, and I'm warning you in case you don't like it either, is that he doesn't believe in what he does; it's all just a game to him. For instance, when he disguises himself as a boy from his school whose head was blown off, knowing that the attack on the school was real made it 300% more incredible.

So far, they've only released six volumes, and if I'm not mistaken, the last one involved him dressing up as a clown and traumatizing a girl to the point where she starts fighting against society's evils. I know that my explanation of the 6th volume might be hard to understand. But that's partly my point—to pique your curiosity so you'll go read it yourself.
